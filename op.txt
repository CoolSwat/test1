<h2 class="heading_1">How We Make the furniture you love.</h2>
<span class="heading_2">Assured quality control - from sourcing of wood, upto doorstep delivery.</span>
<div id="making_process_wrapper">
<div id="making_process_slideshow">
<div class="making_process_slide" style="background-image: url('http://i1.ppfry.com/media/wysiwyg/banners/furniture_made_with_love_1_16_jan.jpg');">
<div class="making_table">
<div class="making_table_cell">
<div><span class="title_1">wood procurement</span>
<p>The Solid Wood lumber [Mango, Sheesham or Teak] needs to be procured at an optimum level of dryness to avoid infestation. All raw materials are sourced by manufacturers from the pre-approved suppliers only to ensure standardisation and best quality.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="making_process_slide" style="background-image: url('http://i1.ppfry.com/media/wysiwyg/banners/furniture_made_with_love_2_16_jan.jpg');">
<div class="making_table">
<div class="making_table_cell">
<div><span class="title_1">Cutting &amp; Panel Making</span>
<p>The rough lumber is then sliced into selected length to match the furniture specifications. These panels are then glued and are placed into a pneumatic clamp carrier where constant and even pressure insures only the best joints.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="making_process_slide" style="background-image: url('http://i1.ppfry.com/media/wysiwyg/banners/furniture_made_with_love_3_16_jan.jpg');">
<div class="making_table">
<div class="making_table_cell">
<div><span class="title_1">Sanding &amp; Finishing</span>
<p>After designated drying times, sanding is performed. Sanding helps eliminate any scratches, cuts, or flaws in the wood. Sanding is also needed in order to ensure that a wood stain soaks in to the wood surface properly. After sanding the wood is stained using solvent based strainers as per the colour selected.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="making_process_slide" style="background-image: url('http://i1.ppfry.com/media/wysiwyg/banners/furniture_made_with_love_4_16_jan.jpg');">
<div class="making_table">
<div class="making_table_cell">
<div><span class="title_1">Quality Check</span>
<p>Each piece of furniture is then quality checked for dimensions, design accuracy, finish quality, raw materials used and exactness of the wood colour at the manufacturers location.</p>
</div>
</div>
